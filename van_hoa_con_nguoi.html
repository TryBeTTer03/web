Diverse Culture and Friendly People

  • Geography

    Hoi An City is located on the northern bank of the lower Thu Bon River. Hoi An is an ancient city of Vietnam, about 795 km south of Hanoi, about 940 km from Ho Chi Minh City, about 122 km from Hue City, about 30 km southeast of Da Nang City, with geographical location:

    - The East borders the East Sea
    - The West and North borders Dien Ban Town
    - The South borders Duy Xuyen District.

  • Diverse Culture and Friendly People

    Hoi An is a melting pot of cultures with a blend of Vietnamese, Chinese, Japanese, and French influences.

    Hoi An is not only beautiful but also rich in cultural values. Here, you can participate in traditional festivals, explore long-standing craft villages such as Thanh Ha Pottery Village, Kim Bong Carpentry Village, and even make your own lanterns.

    The people of Hoi An are warm and hospitable, always ready to welcome you with a bright smile.
